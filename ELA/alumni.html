VIT-AP University is a private university located in Amaravati, the capital of Andhra Pradesh, India. It is the first university located in the capital region. With a history of 32 years of innovaion in educational and research domains. VIT has been a forerunner in delivering quality education. Consistently ranked among the top education institutes in the country, the VIT group of institutions have had a proud tradition of pursuing knowledge and excellence. In keeping with the this tradition, the leadership at VIT-AP resonates a dynamic blend of academic initiative and industry partnership with a vision of creating one of hte finest academic destination in the world.
